ABOUT KELLASH STUDIO

SWAHILI VERSION
🎬 Kellash Studio ni kituo cha kisasa cha burudani, ubunifu na teknolojia, kilichoanzishwa mwaka 2022 na Seth Emmanuel Daniel kwa lengo la kusaidia vipaji vipya na wataalamu kuonesha uwezo wao bila mipaka. Tunatoa huduma mbalimbali zinazojumuisha uzalishaji wa muziki, filamu na video, uchoraji wa picha za kisanii, motion graphics, digital marketing, na usambazaji wa kazi za sanaa mtandaoni.

Kwa Kellash Studio, msanii au mteja ana nafasi ya:
✨ Kufanya kazi na timu yenye ubunifu na uzoefu.
✨ Kupata jukwaa la kusambaza kazi zake kitaifa na kimataifa.
✨ Kujifunza na kupata mafunzo ya kiteknolojia na sanaa.
✨ Kuunganishwa na mashabiki na wateja kupitia mitandao ya kijamii na digital platforms.
Tunajivunia kuwa sehemu ya mabadiliko katika tasnia ya burudani na ubunifu, tukiamini kuwa kila kipaji kina nafasi ya kung’aa.
👉 Kauli mbiu yetu: "WE GIVE YOU UNLIMITED ENTERTAINMENT"

ENGLISH VERSION
🎬 Kellash Studio is a modern center for entertainment, creativity, and technology, founded in 2022 by Seth Emmanuel Daniel with the vision of empowering new talents and professionals to showcase their potential without limits. We offer a wide range of services including music production, film and video creation, artistic design, motion graphics, digital marketing, and online distribution of creative works.

At Kellash Studio, every artist and client has the opportunity to:
✨ Work with a creative and experienced team.
✨ Access a platform to promote and distribute their work locally and internationally.
✨ Learn and gain hands-on experience in art and technology.
✨ Connect with fans, clients, and the global creative community through digital platforms.
We take pride in being part of the transformation in the entertainment and creative industry, believing that every talent deserves a chance to shine.
